About Me

I'm a software developer of over 10 years who started off as an apprentice studying and working. Since then I have had the opportunity to work within every major business sector and have been able to work with a wide range of incredible clients.

Finding the Passion

I started coding and exploring software development when I was 13, I was bored one summer and took the opportunity to learn something new. I fell in love immediately, from then on I started finding any reason I could to build and app or a website for friends and family for any needs that they had.

Going To College

After going through the first year of A-levels I knew that the traditional way of learning was no longer the way I wanted to study. I found a college in a the town over from me and left high school to enrol myself into their apprenticeship course. This was a fantastic way for me to learn, having college based training and then having a business placement to further my learning and to understand real business use.

My First Job

After enrolling on the apprenticeship course I found my placement working with a software house in the nearest city to my hometown. The team was very small, with the boss being a designer and the other member of staff being a backend developer. This gave me an incredible opportunity to throw myself into Frontend Development and have a focus during my study. After just 3 months of being on my work placement, the company extended me a full-time position effective immediately.

Moving to Northern Ireland

When I was 20 I decided I wanted to accomplish some life goals, I decided to get married and moved to a different country. Belfast was the city of choice after several incredible holidays I had the few years previously. Moving here was a complete change in culture and work-ethic. I found a job working as a Senior Software Engineer after just a week of living in the country. Here I learnt a lot under the guidance of a very talented developer. Building applications that benefited the UK police and government - allowing them to detect threats and terrorism acts that people were talking about online.

A Change Of Scenery

After working with this security company for over a year, I decided it was time to move and find something more in keeping with my personal interests. I found a new position working in another software house. This company focused on building web-apps and mobile apps that would allow their customers to handle internal company functions. This included apps that allowed better delivery route planning / Timesheet systems and warehouse inventory management tools. I had the opportunity here to fly to locations throughout Europe and meet the clients face-to-face, gather their requirements, and then build the plans for how we would present them a solution.

Having a Child

After the birth of my Daughter I decided it was again time to find a new position and push myself further, to learn more about software and to move up the seniority ladder. I moved to a company that built a Crypto Trading platform. This was a global operation that processed millions of trades every single day. My role here was to produce the client and internal systems to allow our users to trade, manage their accounts and to perform admin related services including reporting. Given the size of this system I had to drive a lot of change to ensure the application could withstand such a huge volume of traffic at any given time. In this position I took on more of a full-stack role, helping out the backend team with architecture and building logic, and also managing the dev-ops related tasks to ensure the system was scalable and secure.

My Current Postion

After a year and half of working within the Crypto team, I was offered a position in a new company where I would be managing my own team and have the scope to be in a higher position than my current team could allow. I am currently working within a frontend team as the Staff level engineer, making improvements to the existing architecture, driving new feature work and supporting / mentoring new members of staff. The company itself provides a tool that allows its clients to take out a global automated, AI driven security system that protects them from threats made to their e-commerce business.
